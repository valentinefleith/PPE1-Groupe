What is unclaimed money? If a business, financial institution, or government owes you money that you did not collect, it is considered unclaimed money or property. You may be able to file for unclaimed money owed to you or that was owed to a deceased relative if you are their legal heir. Search by state Most unclaimed money is held by state governments from sources such as bank accounts, insurance policies, or state agencies. Search for unclaimed money from your [34]state’s unclaimed property office.
